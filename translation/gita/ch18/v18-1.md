<h1>
Arjuna said: O Great Armed One, I wish to learn of [renunciation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sannyasa) and
[abandonment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ty%C4%81ga) separately, O bright haired one, killer of the Keshi demon.
